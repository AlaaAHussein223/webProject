Why Reading

Reading is a fundamental skill that opens doors to knowledge,
imagination, and personal growth. It enhances vocabulary,
improves focus and concentration, and stimulates creativity.
Reading also reduces stress, improves empathy, and broadens
perspectives by exposing readers to different cultures and ideas.
Overall, reading is a powerful tool for lifelong learning and
self-improvement.
